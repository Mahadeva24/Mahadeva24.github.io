F.A.Q

Frequently Asked Questions

  • What is SaveMate?

    SaveMate is a gift card buying platform that helps you save money on your purchases and invests your cash back into stocks.

  • How does SaveMate work?

    SaveMate allows you to purchase gift cards for various brands and retailers at a discounted rate. When you use the gift card to make a purchase, you earn cash back, which is automatically invested into stocks of your choice.

  • Can i withdraw cash reward in my bank account directly from the wallet?

    Yes ! You can directly withdraw cash rewards to your bank account after you meet the minimum balance of ₹ 999 . Till then you can keep your money invested into stocks for growing your money .

  • Is there a fee for using SaveMate?

    No SaveMate does not charge you anything for use .
